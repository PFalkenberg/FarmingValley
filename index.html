Welcome!

You are a farmer that has just landed on a newly discovered exoplanet. This planet is entirely yours to do with as you please. There is an incredible amount of fertile land to grow crops, as well as plenty of space for future expansion and development. You will start out with some crops that you have brought with you from Earth. Perhaps in the future you can discover new crops that have yet to be found. Unfortunately, you spent your entire life savings to get here. All you have left is $10 and your drive to own the very best farming planet! To get started, begin tilling some land and plant your very first crop. You're sure to make your savings back in no time! Good luck!

I would like to give a special thanks to Rose for creating all of the pixel art used in the game!
